About Me

Professional Overview

I am a Professor in the Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences at North South University, Bangladesh. With a Ph.D. from Niigata University of Pharmacy and Applied Life Sciences (NUPALS), Japan, and postdoctoral training at prestigious institutions including the University of Illinois at Chicago and the University of North Dakota, I bring extensive expertise in drug discovery and molecular pharmacology.

My research focuses on developing novel therapeutic agents for neurological, cardiovascular, and oncological disorders using a combination of computational and experimental approaches. I have a strong background in Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD), molecular dynamics simulations, and natural product chemistry.

As an educator, I teach a range of courses including Pharmacology, Medicinal Chemistry, and Clinical Pharmacokinetics. I have supervised numerous undergraduate and graduate research projects and have a proven track record in securing research grants and publishing in high-impact journals.
