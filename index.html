Overview

Professor in the Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences at North South University, specializing in drug discovery, molecular pharmacology, and medicinal chemistry. With a Ph.D. from Niigata University of Pharmacy and Applied Life Sciences (NUPALS), Japan, and postdoctoral training at prestigious institutions including the University of Illinois at Chicago and the University of North Dakota.
